What is MusicLessonPlanner?

MusicLessonPlanner is a scheduling and organization application aimed at private music teachers. It makes keeping track of a studio of students much easier by managing lesson scheduling, student contact information, and notes from individual lessons.

About the developers

The core development team for MusicLessonPlanner is a group of 6 students from UMass Amherst who are working on the application as a group project for their software engineering course.
